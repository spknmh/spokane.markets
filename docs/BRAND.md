Here's a **warm community, modern PNW** brand direction for **spokane.market**—built around Spokane's vibe: evergreen + river + harvest + craft paper.

## Brand concept

**"Cedar & Harvest"**
Warm, local, handcrafted energy with modern UI structure. Your photography provides the "life"; your UI provides trust and clarity.

---

## Core palette (production-ready)

### Neutrals (warm, readable, modern)

* **Cream (Background)**: `#FBF7F0`
* **Paper (Surface/Card)**: `#F4EEE3`
* **Fog (Subtle UI / borders)**: `#E5DDD1`
* **Charcoal (Text primary)**: `#1F2422`
* **Ash (Text secondary)**: `#4A5450`

### Brand colors (PNW identity)

* **Cedar Green (Primary)**: `#1F4D3A`
* **Moss (Secondary)**: `#5F8C6A`
* **River (Link/Info accent)**: `#2F6F7E`

### Warm accents (community, food, craft)

* **Terracotta (Highlights/Badges)**: `#C86B4A`
* **Honey (CTA accent / success warmth)**: `#D6A23A`

### Status colors (still warm, not "enterprise neon")

* **Success**: `#2E7D5B`
* **Warning**: `#B7791F`
* **Danger**: `#B5423A`
* **Info**: `#2F6F7E`

---

## How to use it (so it stays modern, not rustic)

### Buttons

* **Primary button**: Cedar Green `#1F4D3A` with cream text
* **Secondary**: Paper `#F4EEE3` with Cedar text + Fog border
* **Accent/Seasonal CTA** (sparingly): Honey `#D6A23A` (for "Get reminders", "Subscribe", "Vendor signup")

### Background structure

* App background: **Cream**
* Cards/sections: **Paper**
* Borders/dividers: **Fog**
* Don't use pure white. It kills the warmth.

### Links + interactive

* Links: **River**
* Hover states: slightly deepen River or add underline. Keep it calm.

---

## Typography recommendations (modern + friendly)

* **Headings:** `Fraunces` (warm, local, artsy but still premium)
* **Body/UI:** `Inter` (clean, modern, readable)

If you want *zero personality risk* (still warm): use **`Sora` for headings + Inter body**.

---

## UI styling tokens (Tailwind/shadcn-friendly)

* **Radius:** 14–18px (soft modern)
* **Shadow:** subtle, low contrast (avoid heavy drops)
* **Spacing:** generous vertical rhythm (warmth comes from breathing room)
* **Icons:** rounded stroke icons (Lucide works well)

---

## Voice and microcopy (this matters)

* Use community language:

  * "This weekend in Spokane"
  * "Save this vendor"
  * "Get market reminders"
  * "Support local makers"
* Avoid corporate terms like "portal", "solution", "workflow".

---

## Suggested "brand moments"

* **Seasonal badges** using Terracotta/Honey (e.g., "Spring Market", "Holiday Pop-Up")
* A **"Local Spotlight"** section that uses Paper background + warm photo + simple CTA

---

## Dark mode

Dark mode uses cooler variants of the palette (deeper charcoal backgrounds, muted greens) while preserving warm accents (Honey, Terracotta) for CTAs and highlights. This keeps the brand recognizable and avoids a cold, clinical feel.
