# Spokane Markets — Brand & Color Palette

## Spokane & PNW Inspired (No Blue)

Colors drawn from Spokane's official identity and Pacific Northwest landscape.

| Token | Hex | Usage |
|-------|-----|-------|
| **Primary** | `#006838` | Spokane official green (evergreen, Inland NW) |
| **Accent** | `#d97706` | Amber / wheat (harvest, sunshine) |
| **Going** | `#15803d` | Vibrant green — "I'm going" button |
| **Interested** | `#d97706` | Amber — "Interested" button |

## Icon

- **Brand**: Store icon (lucide-react) — represents markets and local commerce
- **Venues**: MapPin (admin only) — for location/venue management

## Typography

- **Font**: DM Sans — friendly, readable, community feel
